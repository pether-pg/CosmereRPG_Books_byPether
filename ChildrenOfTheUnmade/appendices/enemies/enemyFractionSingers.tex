\section{Singers}
\label{enemySinger}

Millennia ago, Singer armies fought against Honor and his human forces. After their defeat, they were stripped of their forms and forced into slavery as parshmen.

Years passed and now nobody remembers what happened back then. However, Singers' hatred for their enemies never extinguished. Now, with the Everstorm they are reborn into forms lost to time, ready to have their revenge.

Singers' skin looks like a marble, with red patterns on a white or black surface. Depending on the current form, a Singer can have a carapace - either ornamental or serving as an armor.

\tacticsBox{
	Singer troops tend to work in warpairs, two warriors fighting as one. They often use aggressive tactics, striking hard and fast, utilizing abilities granted them by their forms.
}

\tacticsBox{
	\enemySingerHaevenlyOne{}s are the nobility among the Singers. They seldom join the fight, sending their troops instead. However, if the situation calls for a rapid air support, they will be there, enjoying every second of fight against the most hated humans.
}

\section{Running This Chapter}

The characters travel to Vedenar, to use Oathgate there and get to Urithiru. They arrive there in a middle of a serial murder crisis. They need to support investigation efforts of \npcShallan{} to gain trust of the Urithiru Radiants.

\subsection{Character Advancement}

The characters start this chapter at level 10. Advance them twice in this chapter: once after finding the source of mysterious murders and once after they resolve a threat of an Unmade lurking in the city.

\section{Other Allies}
\label{sec:moreAllies}

Apart from potential patrons described in \fullref{sec:dalinarVsArdents} and \fullref{sec:jasnahVsSonsOfHonor}, there are more NPCs in the Tower who can support the party.

Below you can find some more examples you can include in your story. In each of the cases, make sure only the one listed in \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherNoBacking} are present in that scene. The PCs should make more time to secure additional patrons and allies.

\reasonBox{
	\fullref{ssec:mysteriousViolenceBarFight} and following it \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherNoBacking} offer the PCs to face \npcMidnightMother{} even if they didn't complete the Endeavor to find her. You should not reward incomplete quests with possibility of additional allies. If the party wishes to have more troops during that fight, they will need to do more than just following \npcShallan{}. 
}




\subsection{\npcKaladin{}}

During a Highstorm several days after \fullref{ssec:mysteriousViolenceBarFight}, \npcKaladin{} Stormblessed returns to \locationUrithiru{} from his trip to Alethkar. This makes him available for the PCs as a potential ally.

\subsubsection{Patron's Goal}

\npcKaladin{} might offer goals like the following:

\scriptedOption{I Will Protect}{Protect those, who cannot protect themselves. Defend innocents from Midnight creatures or other dangers.}

\subsubsection{Patron Benefit}

As an ally, \npcKaladin{} can fly the party to any reasonable location on Roshar. He can also accept a PC as a squire.

If allied to the PC, \npcKaladin{} will join them during \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherConfront}.





\subsection{\npcRenarin{} Kholin}

Quiet, shy and awkward, \npcRenarin{} is the youngest son of \npcDalinar{} Kholin. He is also a Knight Radiant, bonded to a spren Enlightened by \npcSjaAnat{}.

\subsubsection{Patron's Goal}

\npcRenarin{} might offer goals like the following:

\scriptedOption{Touched by the Unmade}{Learn and teach what does it mean for you to be bonded with one of \npcSjaAnat{}'s children.}

\subsubsection{Patron Benefit}

As an ally, \npcRenarin{} can show you a vision of your future, granting a PC \abilityFutureKnowledgeName[1]:

\abilityFutureKnowledge[1]

He will also join the party to support his allies during \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherConfront}.



\subsection{\npcNavani{} Kholin}

Mother of \npcJasnah{} and \npcElhokar{}, \npcNavani{} was a wife to \npcDeadKing{}, but she recently remarried to \npcDalinar{}. As a scholar, she is fascinated by fabrials and she uses her influence to support artibrabrians in \locationUrithiru{}

\subsubsection{Patron's Goal}

\npcNavani{} might offer goals like the following:

\scriptedOption{Ancient Engineering}{Search for ancient fabrials lost in \locationUrithiru{} and learn how do they work.}

\subsubsection{Patron Benefit}

As a patron, \npcNavani{} can provide support and resources for aspiring artifabrians.

She will also equip the party with a fabrial of their choosing for the fight in \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherConfront}.




\subsection{\npcAdolin{} Kholin}

\npcAdolin{} Kholin, son of \npcDalinar{} is considered one of the most skilled Shardblade duelists in Alethkar, if not the entire world, making him a great potential ally for the party

\subsubsection{Patron's Goal}

\npcAdolin{} might offer goals like the following:

\scriptedOption{Teacher and Student}{Train sword dueling, both as a student and a teacher, to increase your skill with the weapon.}

\subsubsection{Patron Benefit}

As an ally, \npcAdolin{} can train the PCs in a Shardblade fight. He can also always provide helpful suggestion about trends and fashion.

When allied to the party, he will rally to the battle with \npcMidnightMother{} during \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherConfront}.




\subsection{Other}

If other allies or patrons would make sense in your story, feel free to allow the party to compete for their support. Design fitting goals for them and unique support ability to unlock during \fullref{ssec:middnightMotherAllies}.

Whoever you add, make sure the party won't be able to fulfill their goals before \fullref{ssec:mysteriousViolenceBarFight} scene.



\subsection{Level-up!}

After the PCs secure at least one NPC as their patron, advance them to the next level. Subsequent allies and patrons do not grant additional levels.

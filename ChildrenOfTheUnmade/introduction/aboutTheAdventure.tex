\section{About the Adventure}


\subsection{Campaign Themes}

This campaign is using themes as listed below. Use this list to determine if you are interested in running this kind of campaign. In the game, you can encounter themes of:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Corrupted spren
	\item Deals with the devil
	\item Lesser evil
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Campaign Setting}

The story starts in Kholinar ruled by \npcQueen{}, shortly before the arrival of Everstorm. The characters will spend roughly half of the story in here. Later, they will travel through Shadesmar to reach Kharbranth and later - Urithiru.

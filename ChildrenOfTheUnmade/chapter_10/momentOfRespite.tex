\section{A Moment of Respite}
\label{sec:momentOfRespite}

Every few scenes, present the party with something less challenging. Instead, have them visit a location in which they can take a Long Rest and perhaps do some shopping. 

If the PCs own \lootScadrialCoin{} (or other unique currency) allow them to play a scene in which they can spend it.



\subsection{Example Scene}

You can start such a scene with the following:

\quoteBox{
	You find yourself in green, lush valley. The plants in here look like frozen in time, not moving or retreating when touched. Serene, but strange... \\ \\
	You wear a simple, brown clothing. All around, you can see strange, four-legged creatures. They look like a horned swines with a white, curly fur.
}

A PC with a proper expertise may recognize they are in Shinivar. There is little to do in here. The party can rest and enjoy the peaceful area.

\section{Backstory Flashbacks}
\label{sec:backstoryFlashback}

Does a PC have a great backstory that you couldn't fully use in the story? Or perhaps there is an event in there that feels really inspiring, but since it already happened you couldn't make an encounter out of it? Now is your last chance!



\subsection{Example Scene}

You can start such a scene with something similar to:

\quoteBox{
	You are standing on a wall, wearing a uniform from a days long gone. In the field before you an army prepares for battle. "Blackthorn is here!", someone shouts. \\ \\
	You look around. This place, this moment, they feel familiar. Shortly after, you realize - this is a day when Rathalas fell. This is a day you lost your family in flames...
}

Le the PCs face a moment from their past. What will they do? Will they enjoy the brief moment with their family they are given? Or will they do something differently, knowing very well it can't change the past? Or will they freeze and despair?

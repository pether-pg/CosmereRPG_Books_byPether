\section{Feral Spren}
\label{enemyWildSpren}

People on Roshar have known the spren their entire life. They always appeared nearby plants, fire or strong emotions. Small entities made of glowing light, immaterial and harmless, could never harm anyone.

In the Shadesmar, those spren are more akin to animals. They can be bred and domesticated, but they can also roam the wilderness of the Cognitive Realm, attacking unsuspecting travelers.

Luckily, in here they can also be hit with conventional weapons. Though they won't kill them, they are often enough to make them retreat from the fight.

\tacticsBox{
	Feral Spren are acting on instinct, being attracted to an emotion or natural phenomena. In the fight they don't strategize. Instead, they focus on the thing that got their attention, often to the detriment of the person responsible for it.
}

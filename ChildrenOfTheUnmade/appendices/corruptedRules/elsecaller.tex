\section{Enlightened Elsecaller}

\corruptedBelief{I will reach my potential.}{I will reject imperfection.}

\subsection{Philosophy Changes}

Aiming for ones full potential is never enough for Elsecallers influenced by \npcSjaAnat{}. Instead their goal is absolute perfection. They despise anything imperfect, rejecting it from their lives.

At their best, they reach an excellence in each task they set for themselves.

At their worst, they grow hateful and resentful towards everyone and everything, including themselves. They can never be satisfied by anything and they will punish any imperfection in themselves and others.

\subsection{Enlightened Inkspren}

In the Physical Realm, the Enlightened inkspren manifest as small iridescent person, capable of changing their size at will.

In the Cognitive Realm they look very similar, though they are always in their full size.

\subsection{Drawbacks and Complications}

Abilities granted by Enlightened talents cause the Elsecaller to be split between Physical and Cognitive Realms. At most of the time they can control it, but the GM may request a Discipline test from them (\corruptedResistanceDC{}). On a failure, they are lost for a few moments in the Shadesmar, unable to assist the rest of the team and exposed to feral spren attacks.

After a few rounds they are always transported back to the Physical Realm.

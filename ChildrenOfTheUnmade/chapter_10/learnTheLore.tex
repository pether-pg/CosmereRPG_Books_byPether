\section{Learn the Lore}
\label{sec:learnTheLore}

Some players (or their PCs) may enjoy discovering the lore of the world more than fighting or roleplaying. You may have a scene dedicated for them as well.

\textbf{Note:} When you do, please be careful not to include any spoilers for people who do not wish to experience them!



\subsection{Example Scene}

You can have such a scene starting with something like:

\quoteBox{
	You are standing on a battlefield, victorious. Your enemies are dead and gone. Despite heavy loses, the day is won. \\ \\
	You see a shaken man approaching you. "Jezerien", he says, "I can't return this time. I can't!"
}

The PCs experience the Final Desolation and the heralds abandoning their Oathpact. One of the PCs takes a role of Jezerien, while the others are common soldiers. The PCs can learn a bit more about that key moment in the history of Roshar.

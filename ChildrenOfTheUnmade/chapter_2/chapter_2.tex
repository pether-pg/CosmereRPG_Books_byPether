\section{Running This Chapter}

In this chapter, public execution of \npcRebelArdent{} starts city riots. During those, the character will sooner or later need to find somewhere safe to find. At that moment, they will meet with \npcSjaAnat{}, who will set their objectives for the future.

\subsection{Character Advancement}

The characters start this chapter at level 2. Advance them to level 3 after they meet \npcSjaAnat{}.

The following events may affect their path to radiance:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Attempting to free \npcRebelArdent{}
	\item Defending people during riots
	\item Bonding \npcSjaAnat{}'s children
\end{itemize}


\subsection{Forging Alliances}

The following actions can help the characters to become allies with Kholinar fractions:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Defending ardents during the riots
	\item Helping palace guard defend the palace
\end{itemize}

\section{Dramatis Person\ae}
\label{personae}

Following NPCs and fractions appear in the adventure:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcTroublemaker[s]{} - adolescent woman, most probably friend or family of the PCs; enthusiastic and impulsive, prone to getting into trouble.
	
	\item \npcGrandma[s]{} - \npcTroublemaker{}'s grandmother, grumpy and fast to insult.
	
	\item \npcRebelArdent[s]{} - ardent of Devotary of Denial, accused \npcQueen{} of ten foolish attributes; her execution sparks the riots in Kholinar.
	
	\item \npcLockedArdent[s]{} - ardent, friend of \npcRebelArdent{}, got locked up after her imprisonment.
	
	\item \npcHoid[s]{} - nobody really important...
\end{itemize}



\subsubsection{Royal Palace}

The Palace closed its doors to the hardships troubling the city. Only queen's most trusted allies have access inside.

Following NPCs are related to this fraction:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcQueen[s]{} - wife of \npcElhokar{}, at his absence she rules the city; bonded to \npcYeligNar{}, the \npcYeligNarTitle{} she got her soul corrupted and her judgment clouded.
	
	\item \npcElhokar[s]{} - king of Alethkar, currently located in the Shattered Plains.
	
	\item \npcDeadKing[s]{} - former king of Alethkar, father of \npcElhokar{}, killed by the Assasin in White.
\end{itemize}



\subsubsection{\npcFractionCult{}}

Ardents close to \npcQueen{} herself, whose lives full of excess lead to drawing the sight of \npcHeartRevel{}, the \npcHeartRevel{}. Now their minds are lost, following only the beating of the terrible heart.

Following NPCs can be found in this fraction:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcRevelerGuide[s]{} - guides new cultists to their first revelry.
	
	\item \npcRevelerGeneral[s]{} - got attracted to the revelry in the Monastery; her unwillingness to follow \npcHeartRevel{} got her imprisoned.
\end{itemize}



\subsubsection{\npcFractionWall{}}

With \npcQueen{} secluded in her Palace, the responsibility to defend the city fell to the \npcFractionWall{}. 

Following NPCs are included here:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcHighmarshal[s]{} - took charge once the previous commander fell in the city riots; now she does her best to protect the city and its people.
	
	\item \npcSoulcaster[s]{} - an Emerald Soulcaster from the city Monastery, got rescued by \npcHighmarshal{} during the city riots to soulcast food for them.
\end{itemize}



\subsubsection{Singers}

During the Everstorm, Kholinar parshmen are transformed into Singers who devastate the city before establishing a camp beyond its walls.

Following NPCs are included in this group:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcSingerWorkerGranM[s]{} - malen worker at \npcGrandma{}'s.
	
	\item \npcSingerWorkerGranF[s]{} - femalen worker at \npcGrandma{}'s.
	
	\item \npcSingerWouned[s]{} - wounded during the fights in Kholinar.
	
	\item \npcSingerEnvoy[s]{} - Envoyform Singer at the camp.
\end{itemize}




\subsubsection{The Unmade}

Following Unmade are referenced or make an appearance in the adventure:

\begin{itemize}
	\item \npcSjaAnat{}, the \npcSjaAnatTitle{} - eerie, mysterious and secretive, \npcSjaAnat{} subtly arms and leads the PCs, arming them and manipulating them to achieve her own agenda.
	
	\item \npcThrill, the \npcThrillTitle{} - violent and brutal, relishes in the sounds of battle, fueling the rage of rioters and warriors fighting in Kholinar.
	
	\item  \npcDustmother{}, \npcDustmotherTitle{} - gone for millennia, now only \lootDustcaster{} remains as a memento of her terrible power.
	
	\item  \npcHeartRevel{}, the \npcHeartRevel{} - drawn to indulgence and excess, influences \npcFractionCult{} in Kholinar.
	
	\item \npcBlackFisher{}, the \npcBlackFisherTitle{} - since the Scouring of Aimia, only tales and \lootGateOpener{} remain of him.
	
	\item \npcMoelach{} - often found near the dying people, showing them visions of the future known as Death Rattles.
	
	\item \npcMidnightMother{}, the \npcMidnightMotherTitle{} - lurks in the deeps of Urithiru, spawning creatures of horrors to torment its new inhabitants.
	
	\item \npcYeligNar{}, the \npcYeligNarTitle{} - capable of bestowing the power of Surges, he is now bonded to \npcQueen{}, corrupting her soul.
	
	\item \npcMishram{} - once carrying the power to rival \npcOdium{} himself, captured by ancient Radiants, she is now imprisoned in the Spiritual Realm plotting her revenge.
\end{itemize}

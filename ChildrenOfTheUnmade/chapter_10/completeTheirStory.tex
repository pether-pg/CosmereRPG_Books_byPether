\section{Complete Their Story}
\label{sec:completeTheirStory}

This adventure is closing to an end. It is a good moment to ensure the personal stories of the PCs are finished as well. Use those scenes to complete their characters' arcs and provide a satisfying ending to their history.

There is no one way of doing it. You must use your own judgment and familiarity with your PCs' backstories to determine the proper ending. You can also discuss with the player how would they see the fitting scene (assuming they prefer such approach above a surprise served by the GM).




\subsection{Example Scene}

For this scene, you may do something like:

\quoteBox{
	For a brief moment, you feel yourself standing before a mirror: the same face, the same hair, the same smile. But those eyes... Those are your mother's eyes. \\ \\
	Soon, you realize. This is your brother. This is the day he made that terrible choice. The last day you've seen him alive.
}

The PC has a chance to speak with their brother before his fatal departure. Though the actions of the PC cannot change the past, this might be the only chance for the PC to reconcile, change their mind, say proper good bye, or whatever is required for the character to complete their story.

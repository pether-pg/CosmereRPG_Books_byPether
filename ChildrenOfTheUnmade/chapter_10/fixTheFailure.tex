\section{Fix the Failure}
\label{sec:fixTheFailure}

If one of the PCs suffered a particularly devastating failure while attempting one of the challenges, you may allow them to relive that moment and attempt to succeed this time.

It may be a failed Endeavor, unsatisfying Conversation, or a Combat scene in which a beloved NPC died.



\subsection{Example Scene}

You can start such a scene with the following:

\quoteBox{
	You find yourself in a cold underground room illuminated with a bloody light from ruby spheres. A person in white apron stands before you, right next to the patient lying in bed. \\ \\
	"You are capable", he says. "Do the honor, make the cut, know the future and join the Diagram in our quest of protecting the humanity."
}

A player may recognize the scene reenacting one of the past challenges - \fullref{ssec:conclaveHospitalPatient}. The party has a chance to experience this moment again. And perhaps this time they will manage to save the NPC they wanted to protect...
